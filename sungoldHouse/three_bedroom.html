Three Bedroom
Hibiscus Apartment

This three-bedroom fully self contained apartment offers spacious and comfortable living for six. Children are welcome and the apartment is wheelchair accessible.

The master bedroom features a king-size bed (or two twins); air conditioner and ceiling fans; ensuite bathroom with shower. Two other bedrooms - both with king size or two twin beds - share a second bathroom which features tub and shower.

The well furnished open-plan living areas are cooled by ceiling fans, and a fully equipped kitchen provides all the conveniences of home. The dining room seats six and Al fresco dining on the west-facing patio which overlooks the pool deck and garden is an option.

On the pool deck you will find loungers, tables,umbrellas and plunge-pool.

Cable TV and free wireless internet connection, reading material and a selection of board and card games are supplied for your further convenience.

Smoking is NOT permitted. Please see our Rental Terms & Conditions
