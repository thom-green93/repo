One Bedroom
Croton Apartment

The Croton is a very comfortable one-bedroom self contained studio apartment with ensuite bathroom, ideally suited for one to two persons.

The air condtioned bedroom is furnished with a double bed, bedside lamp and built in wardrobe. The bathroom offers a glass enclosed walk-in shower.

The living area features combined living, dining and kitchen area, cooled by ceiling fans. Cable TV and free wireless internet connection, selection of board and card games are supplied for your further convenience.

The kitchen includes micro-wave oven, toaster, kettle, cooker with oven and coffee maker.

Smoking is NOT permitted. Please see our Rental Terms & Conditions
